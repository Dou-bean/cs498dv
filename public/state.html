Case details

Here is the detailed confirmed case composition partitioned as "Deaths", "Infected", and "Recovered".

From the pie chart we can learn that the composition of the three part differs greatly among states. For some states such as Massachusetts, Texas, Pennsylvania, etc have significantly higher recovered ratio, while in many other states the infected is the majority of the confirmed cases. This indicates the degree of hospital saturation and if this state has reached a "turn point".
